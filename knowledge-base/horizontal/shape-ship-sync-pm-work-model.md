## How to Apply

Use Shape/Ship/Sync to audit your PM work distribution:

1. **Map your weekly activities** into the three buckets. Most PMs underestimate how much time goes to Ship and Sync vs. Shape.
2. **For Shape tasks** (strategy, vision, goals, specs, discovery, roadmap, design feedback): actively experiment with AI tools. Strategy and discovery work should increasingly be AI-augmented â€” treat outputs as first drafts to refine.
3. **For Ship tasks** (project management, scope tradeoffs, quality assurance): selectively apply AI to spec writing and release communications. Keep scope-priority tradeoffs human-driven.
4. **For Sync tasks** (stakeholder alignment, team coordination, organizational influence): double down on human skills. These are the PM competencies least likely to be automated and most likely to differentiate.

The model also informs team investment: Shape-heavy work gets AI tooling budget; Sync-heavy work gets training and coaching.
